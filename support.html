Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a workout?

Tap "+ New Workout" on the home screen, enter a name, and tap Create.

How do I add exercises to a workout?

Open a workout and tap "+ New Exercise" at the bottom.

  • Select from the built-in list of exercises, or
  • Create a custom exercise (Premium feature).

How do I track my weight for an exercise?

Tap on any exercise to expand it, enter the weight (and any other details), then tap Log.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Follow these steps on your iPhone:

  1. Open the Settings app
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap Subscriptions
  4. Select Optimal Fitness
  5. Tap Cancel Subscription

How do I delete my account?

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Delete Account

This will permanently remove your account and all associated data including any purchases you may have made.

I purchased Premium but it is not showing

Try these steps:

  1. Close and reopen the app
  2. Go to the paywall and tap "Restore Purchases"
  3. If it is still not working, email us at ahadsapps@icloud.com with your Apple ID email

Troubleshooting

If you run into problems (crashes, data not saving, workouts missing, etc.), please include this when you contact support:

  • The device you are using (for example, iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version
  • The app version
  • A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened
  • Screenshots if possible
