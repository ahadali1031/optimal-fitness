Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a workout?

Tap the + button on the home screen to create a new workout. Give it a name and start adding exercises.

How do I track my weight for an exercise?

Tap on any exercise in your workout to expand it. You can enter your weight, sets, and reps there.

What do the muscle diagram colors mean?

The colors show how intensely each muscle is being worked in your workout. Darker colors indicate more exercises targeting that muscle.

How do I restore my purchase?

Go to Settings → Subscription → Restore Purchases. Your premium status will be restored if you have an active subscription.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. This will permanently remove your account and all associated data.
